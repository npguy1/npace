Our Data Analytics Labs help clients with their most critical asset – Data. Our Data Analytics Labs help clients unlock the true potential of their data. With our Analytics Labs clients can

  • Choose the preferred managed cloud and protocol stack on which the POC will run
  • Integrate and start building analytics around data
  • Quickly spawn different projects cost effectively

Safeway Real time customer data analysis

Netpace engineers are working with the Safeway teams to expedite time taken from the moment data is captured by store POS systems to leveraging that to take critical business decisions. The goal is to rapidly analyze customer purchase information and use it for various customer and enterprise use cases.

Use Cases
  • Updating purchase history in customer loyalty app on a real time basis
  • Store managers and employees see real time sales reports and make faster marketing, supply chain and logistical decisions
  • Send coupons and other store promotions based on customer purchase history and encourage more frequent store visits

vNitro the Next Generation SDN Management Platform vNitro is a management platform for Virtual and Software Defined Networks. Out of the box APIs and templates for automated network discovery, remediation, analytics and migration for enterprises and service providers.

Platform Offerings
  • Real-Time network analytics and business metrics
  • Automatic remediation from catastrophic network events
  • P->V/SDN Migration
  • Support for all major vendors Cisco, VMWare, Juniper, F5, HP, Dell, Citrix, and more..
  • Extensive networks templates for Virtual and SDN networks
  • Out of the box multi-vendor support
  • Web based intuitive fast and simple UI
  • Extensible API library
  • Built on a Big Data Analytics platform
