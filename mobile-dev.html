Mobile App Development

Let us take your mobile strategy to the next level Native, Custom & Hybrid apps

Our mobile development and consulting services have helped our clients from conceptualizing their applications to publishing them on app stores and tracking their performance. Apart from undertaking client and server side development we integrate apps with existing enterprise wide used system through SOAP & REST web services. We make the most of all available mobile technologies including geo-location, geo-fencing, QR code scanning and other location based services.

Testing is an integral part of any app development and our teams can help your organization test across platforms, devices, OSes and browsers. We have experience working on both customer facing and enterprise apps using native code or by leveraging mobile development platforms such as Apache Cordova.

We provide complete mobile development services:
  • App Concept & Design
  • App Navigation
  • Native & Hybrid applications
  • B2C & B2E applications
  • iOS, Android & Windows development
  • Test using real and virtual devices
  • Application publishing
  • Application monitoring and analytics

Safeway B2E Mobile App

Netpace designers and engineers are working with the Safeway Mobile Innovation Team to build both customer and employee facing mobile apps for over 1400 Safeway stores across the US. The Club Card App which is available on iPad helps Safeway to reduce the amount of time customers take to sign up for their loyalty program. The Enterprise App allows Safeway store employees and managers undertake their day-to-day tasks using their iPhones.

  • Easy 2 step process to onboard new customers to the customer loyalty program
  • Employees can sign in using their employee login credentials used in LDAP
  • Ability to scan physical club cards using front and back iPad cameras
  • Both the iPad and iPhone apps integrate with Safeway's current backend system and infrastructure
