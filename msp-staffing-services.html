MSP & Staffing Services

Individually tailored, attentive level of service

A Managed Service Program, or MSP, is a highly effective and efficient way for employers to manage their contingent workforce.

At Netpace, we offer long-term vision and proactively manage infrastructures which help businesses scale and grow. We partner with our clients to understand their needs and help analyze how we can improve their vision. Through our bespoke programs, we create specifically tailored specialized services and offerings for our clients and partners.

The COVID-19 pandemic across the globe and the rising percentage of contingent workers has harbored in a new reality for businesses. Businesses and organizations need to quickly adapt their talent acquisition strategy to fit the new reality. Our quick adaption of the changing talent landscape is another reason why you should engage with Netpace's MSP program.

Thanks to decades of experience in the MSP arena, Netpace brings with itself a host of benefits to the partners we are engaging with. These include, but are not limited to: technology and market expertise, process efficiencies, ease of scalability and compliant practices, as well as cost savings.

  • We are a Minority Owned Business, certified by NMSDC
  • We offer services in the United States as well as across multiple international territories
  • Our data-centric services and solutions are used to drive digital transformations and empower our customers to make highly informed business decisions
  • We are a preferred supplier partner of MSP/VMS programs within KellyOCG, Yoh, Workforce Logiq and various MSP programs globally
